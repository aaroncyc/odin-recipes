Aaron's Famous Brownie Recipe

This is Aaron's house famous brownie recipe that is absolutely to die for. A decadent and sweet brownie that serves 4 people, or 1 if you're someone like me.

This recipe only takes a couple minutes to prepare and tastes infinitely better than the box mixes.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Granulated Sugar (200g)
  • 1 Cup Brown Sugar (200g)
  • 3 eggs (room temperature)
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder (75g)
  • 1 cup flour (120g)
  • 1 teaspoon salt (6g)
  • 1.5 cup dark chocolate chips (260g)
  • 1 cup melted butter (225g)
  • 1 tsbp coffee (15mL)
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract (15mL)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F and prep a 8x8-inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine melted butter, eggs, vanilla extract, and coffee then mix together well.
  3. Add the white and brown sugars to the mixture and whisk together well.
  4. Sift the cocoa powder, flour, and salt into the bowl and mix until just combined.
  5. Toss in chocolate chips (or chunks) and fold into the batter.
  6. Add batter topan and smooth to the edges. You can add more chocolate chips to the top before baking if desired.
  7. Bake for about 30 minutes or until the center is just set. You'll noitce the center does not wiggle when the pan is moved.
